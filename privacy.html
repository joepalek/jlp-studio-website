1. Who We Are

LightworkAI ("we," "us," or "our") is a sole-proprietor AI automation and custom software services business. We build workflow automation tools, web applications, and AI-powered systems for small and medium businesses.

This privacy policy applies to our website, our contact form, our social media business accounts, and any automated messaging systems we operate on third-party platforms (Facebook, Instagram, and others).

Business contact: LightworkAI — contact via the intake form at lightworkai.com/contact

2. Information We Collect

Information you give us directly

When you fill out our contact/intake form, you may provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name or type
  • A description of your business problem or project
  • A budget range (optional)

We only collect what you voluntarily submit. No form fields are required beyond name, email, and a description of your situation.

Information from social media platforms

When you send a message or comment on our Facebook Page or Instagram Business Account, the platform may share with us:

  • Your public profile name and profile photo
  • Your platform user ID
  • The content of messages you send to us
  • The content of comments you post on our content
  • Timestamps of those interactions

We do not receive your phone number, email address, or any private profile information solely from a social media interaction unless you choose to share it.

Automatically collected information

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a static hosting service. We do not run our own web server analytics. GitHub may collect standard server log data (IP addresses, browser type, page requests) pursuant to their own privacy policy.

We do not use tracking pixels, third-party analytics scripts, or advertising networks on this site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information you submit to us to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or message
  • Scope and quote a potential project
  • Communicate with you about an active engagement
  • Send project-related updates and deliverables

We do not use your information to:

  • Send marketing emails or newsletters without your explicit consent
  • Build advertising profiles
  • Sell or rent your information to third parties
  • Train AI models on your business information

4. Social Media Interactions

LightworkAI operates business accounts on Facebook and Instagram. We use Meta's API and platform tools to monitor and respond to messages and comments sent to these accounts.

Automated responses

Our social media accounts may use automated systems to send initial responses to common inquiries (e.g., questions about our services, pricing, or process). These automated systems:

  • Only respond to messages and comments that you initiate
  • Will identify themselves as automated if you ask directly
  • Do not send unsolicited messages to people who have not contacted us
  • Log interaction details (platform, timestamp, message summary) to an internal file for quality review

If your message indicates a real business need or project interest, it is escalated to a human (Joe) for personal follow-up. Automated responses are limited to general inquiries.

Social platform data policies

When you interact with our accounts on Facebook or Instagram, your interaction is also governed by Meta's Privacy Policy (facebook.com/policy). We access only the data Meta makes available to Page operators through their approved API permissions.

What we do with social message data

  • Message content is used only to generate a relevant response
  • Qualified lead information is kept in a private internal file for follow-up
  • We do not sell, share, or publish message content
  • We retain message logs for up to 90 days for quality review purposes

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties except:

  • Service providers: We may use Supabase (database hosting) to store contact form submissions. Supabase processes data under their own privacy policy and data processing agreement.
  • Platform operators: When you interact with us on Facebook or Instagram, Meta processes that interaction data under Meta's policies.
  • Legal requirements: We will disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process.

Any future third-party integrations will be disclosed in updates to this policy.

6. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for the duration of any potential or active business relationship, and up to 2 years after last contact.
  • Social media interaction logs: Retained for up to 90 days.
  • Project-related communications: Retained for up to 5 years for record-keeping purposes.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below).

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we hold:

  • Access to internal files and databases is restricted to LightworkAI personnel
  • We use HTTPS for all web traffic
  • API credentials are stored in configuration files not exposed publicly

No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take appropriate precautions given the scale and sensitivity of data we handle.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Opt out of any future communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the intake form at lightworkai.com/contact. We will respond within 10 business days.

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR. If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA. We honor these rights regardless of where you are located.

9. Cookies

Our website itself does not set cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or session cookies.

GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) may set cookies as part of their infrastructure. Google Fonts (used for typography) may log requests to their servers. Both are governed by their own privacy policies.

If we add analytics or other third-party scripts in the future, we will update this policy and add appropriate cookie consent mechanisms.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to businesses and business owners, not to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our services or legal requirements change. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our website or services after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Contact form: lightworkai.com/contact
  • Business: LightworkAI
  • Location: Illinois / Tennessee, United States

We respond to privacy inquiries within 10 business days.
