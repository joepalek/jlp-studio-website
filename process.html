How it works

Simple process. No surprises.

From your first message to final delivery — here's exactly what happens.

01

Tell us your problem

Fill out our intake form. No sales call required first. Just describe what's broken, slow, or costing you money. Be as specific or as vague as you need to be — we'll ask the right questions from there.

We read every submission. You'll hear back from a person, not an automated sequence.

Go to intake form
02

We scope it honestly

We'll tell you what's actually buildable, what it costs, and how long it takes. If the problem isn't worth automating — if the manual solution is faster and cheaper — we'll tell you that too.

We don't upsell. We don't inflate scope to run up the bill. A short project that actually works is worth more to both of us than an expensive project that doesn't.

Response within 1 business day
03

Escrow for large projects

For projects over $3,000 we use escrow. Your payment is deposited with a neutral third party — not us. The funds are only released when you confirm that what we built matches what we agreed to build.

Smaller projects use a simple 50% milestone structure: half up front, half on delivery.

Applies to projects $3,000+
04

Delivery + handoff

We build it, test it, and hand it off with written documentation — how it works, how to use it, and how to troubleshoot common issues.

If you want ongoing support after launch, we offer monthly maintenance arrangements. You're never left holding something you don't understand.

Documentation included on every project
Common questions

Things people ask before reaching out

Do I need to know anything about AI?

No. You need to know your business problem. We handle all the technical side. If we use terms you don't understand, ask us to explain — that's on us, not you.

What's the minimum project size?

No hard minimum. Small, focused tools can be very valuable. If it's a quick fix, we'll scope it honestly and quote accordingly — we don't pad small jobs.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

Then the intake form is exactly right for you. Describe what's frustrating or slow. We'll map it back to what's buildable and tell you clearly whether it makes sense to build it.

How do you handle ongoing maintenance?

Monthly arrangements available. We don't lock you in — month-to-month only. If you no longer need us, you keep everything we built. No license fees, no hostage software.

Fill out the intake form. No commitment, no sales pressure.
