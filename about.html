How we work

Rules we follow on every project

  • ✓

    We scope before we quote. No ballpark numbers thrown out to win work and revised upward later. We understand what you need first.

  • ✓

    We tell you if it's not worth building. Sometimes the answer is "don't automate this." If that's true, we say it — and we explain why.

  • ✓

    You own what we build. Source code, documentation, everything. No recurring license, no hostage software, no platform lock-in.

  • ✓

    Escrow on large projects. We put the guarantee in writing and enforce it with a neutral third party. You pay when you confirm delivery.

  • ✓

    One person handles your project. Not handed off to a junior after the sale. Consistent contact, consistent quality.

  • ✓

    Documentation on every delivery. You shouldn't need us to use what we built. The handoff includes how it works and how to maintain it.
