5. Payment and Escrow

Standard projects (under $3,000)

Payment is typically structured as 50% deposit before work begins and 50% upon delivery. Specific payment terms are defined in the project agreement.

Large projects ($3,000 and above)

For projects with a total value of $3,000 or more, LightworkAI uses a neutral third-party escrow service as the default payment structure unless both parties agree otherwise in writing.

Under the escrow structure:

  • The full project fee (or milestone amount) is deposited into escrow before work begins on that milestone
  • Funds are held by the escrow service — not by LightworkAI
  • Funds are released to LightworkAI only upon the client's written confirmation of satisfactory delivery
  • If delivery is disputed, the escrow service's dispute resolution process applies

The specific escrow service used will be identified in the project agreement.

Late payment

Invoices not paid within 30 days of the due date may accrue interest at 1.5% per month. LightworkAI reserves the right to pause or terminate work on unpaid engagements.

Refunds

Refund terms are specified in the project agreement. Deposits for work already completed are generally non-refundable. Escrow dispute resolution provides the primary mechanism for contested deliveries.
